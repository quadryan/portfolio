Shopee - Returns & Refunds UX

Operations Business Analyst Intern

Jun 2025 â€“ Aug 2025 | Regional Operations

Project Scope

Problem Statement: Reducing costs via streamlining of Return and Refund (RR) UX Entry Points.

The returns and refunds process had multiple entry points across the app, leading to unnecessarily high compensation costs. My task was to identify which entry points could be removed or consolidated without negatively impacting customer satisfaction.

An example of a returns and refunds (RR) UX entry point on Shopee's mobile app, that was part of my analysis.

UX Research Process

Quantitative: Competitive Benchmarking
Conducted comprehensive UX audit of returns/refunds flows across Lazada, Tiktok Shop, Taobao etc.
Qualitative: SQL Data Analysis
Queried complex datasets to analyze buyer behavior patterns across different entry points. Identified cost drivers and analyzed correlations with customer satisfaction scores.

Key UX Findings

Redundant Entry Points
Multiple entry points serving the same function resulted in higher instances of users requesting for returns and refunds and potentially created confusion for users.
Marginal Increase in Customer Satisfaction Scores upon adding a specific UX RR Entry point
A/B Testing Results showed that certain entry points did not significantly improve customer satisfaction, indicating potential for removal without negative impact.

Impact: Recommendations had potential to lower compensation rate by up to 10%
